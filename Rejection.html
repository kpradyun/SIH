Eligibility Rules:

  1. Candidates pursing courses through Open Universities.
  2. Student availing benefit of any other scholarship Scheme from any Central/State/UT Government Schemes or any scheme run by any autonomous body under them.
  3. Candidates gaining admission through 'Management Quota'.
  4. Candidates pursuing Diploma course.
  5. Candidates pursuing Post-Graduate level studies, in any discipline.
  6. Candidates having family income over Rs. 8.00 Lakh per annum.
  7. Not applied through online registration in the AICTE Portal.
  8. Taking admission in the Colleges other than the prescribed colleges given in AICTE Portal.
  9. Admission through Agents/NGOs.
