How it works?

Online registration and uploading of documents by students.
Verification of student details by institution.
Forwarding of verified students list to SAG.
Verification and sanctioning of scholarship by SAG.
Forwarding of sanctioned student list to Finance Bureau.
Verification of bank account by Finance Bureau.
Release of amount by Finance Bureau.
